MentorBot was designed by three Hiram College students for the 2016 Kent Hack Enough hackathon. While the original idea for MentorBot was to help victims of cyberbullying deal with the aftermath of cyberbullying is has evovled into an AI designed to help people talk about their problems. MentorBot is someone to talk to when either no one else is there or you want to talk to someone but don't want a real person. We understand that talking about how you feel and the issues you are facing isn't easy so we hope to alieve some of that uncomfortableness with MentorBot. While MentorBot offers unlimited anonymous chat sessions, sessions can be saved and linked to an account at a user's request.
